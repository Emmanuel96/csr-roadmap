CSR Road Map - Where are you?

This exercise is designed to give participants 'snapshot' of where their organization is located on the CSR road map of zero to full engagement. The questions included are generic with some flexibility for individual or specific action to influence the results. For a full list of CSR activity categorised under the CSR-A Four Pillars.
