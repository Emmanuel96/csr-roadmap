Welcome to the CSR-A Road Map - Find Out Where You Are!

This interactive questionnaire is designed to give participants an indicator of where their organisation is located in respect to current CSR engagement and activity.
It will give you a ‘snapshot’ of where you are doing well and where you could be doing better.

How to use the CSR road map...

This road map is intended to give you a quick visual representation of where your organisation sits in regard to socially responsible activity currently undertaken.

The roadmap questions are split into of four sections, environment, workplace, community and philanthropy. These are our Four Pillars of CSR. Each Pillar has 5 questions, with 4 possible responses and an optional 6th input that allows you to score for activity outside the scope of the questions.

Simply respond to the questions by checking the boxes that describes your level of activity. When you tick either Positive Action or Leading Action, the previous boxes will be automatically ticked adding to your overall results.

When you have answered all the questions in each pillar scroll down to the results. You can enter your details here to receive an email which will provide you with a detailed record of your results.
